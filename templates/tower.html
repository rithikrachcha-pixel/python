Tower of Hanoi

Pymetrics Tower Game — move all disks to the right peg in as few moves as possible

How to play
  • Move all disks from the left peg (A) to the right peg (C).
  • You may only move one disk at a time — only the top disk of a peg.
  • You may never place a larger disk on a smaller disk.
  • Click a peg to select it, then click another peg to move the top disk there.
  • The fewer moves you use compared to the optimal, the higher your score!
