Major test providers we simulate

Many banks share the same assessment partner — so practising one provider often unlocks prep for several firms.

AON (CUT-E)

Numerical · Verbal · Logical, Switch

Industry standard. Powers Deutsche Bank, UBS, Wells Fargo and others. Rapid-fire item-level timing.

SHL

Numerical · Verbal · Inductive

Industry standard. Powers Barclays, Goldman Sachs, HSBC. 20–25 min timed sections.

KORN FERRY (TALENT-Q)

Adaptive Numerical & Verbal

Question difficulty adjusts to your answers. Powers HSBC, Citi, Deloitte, Standard Chartered.

CAPPFINITY

Strengths & Situational Judgment

Strengths-based. Powers KPMG, EY, Lloyds, HSBC, BNP Paribas. PST, SKIM and SJT formats.

PYMETRICS (HARVER)

Neuroscience Games

BART, Go/No-Go, N-Back, Digit Span, Attention. Used by JP Morgan, Goldman Sachs, Blackstone.

WATSON GLASER

Critical Thinking

Inferences, assumptions, deductions. Used by Rothschild & Co., Linklaters, Allen & Overy, McKinsey.
