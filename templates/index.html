Tips for Assessment Games

🎯

Be Authentic

Pymetrics games measure your natural traits — trying to "game" them often backfires. Answer instinctively.

⏱️

Practice Speed

SHL tests are timed. Practice until answering quickly feels natural — don't sacrifice accuracy for pace.

🧘

Stay Calm

Anxiety hurts reaction time and working memory. Take a breath before each game.

🔁

Repeat Often

Cognitive assessments improve with practice. Run through each game multiple times over several days.
