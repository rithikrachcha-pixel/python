📋 How It Works

  • A sequence of digits will appear one at a time.
  • Forward Span: Type the digits in the same order you saw them.
  • Backward Span: Type the digits in reverse order.
  • Sequences get longer each round until you fail twice at the same length.
  • Your span score is the longest sequence you recalled correctly.
