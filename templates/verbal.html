📋 Instructions

  • Read each passage carefully, then decide if the statement is True, False, or Cannot Say.
  • True — the statement follows logically from the passage.
  • False — the statement contradicts the passage.
  • Cannot Say — you cannot determine truth or falsity from the passage alone.
  • Base your answer only on the text — do not use outside knowledge.
  • You have 60 seconds per question. Answering within 20 seconds earns a time bonus.
💡 Tip: "Cannot Say" is very common in SHL tests. If the passage neither confirms nor denies, Cannot Say is correct — even when the statement seems obviously true from general knowledge.
