Money Exchange Game

Pymetrics Trust Game — how much do you trust a stranger?

How it works
  • You will play 5 rounds with an anonymous partner.
  • Each round you receive $10. Decide how much to send to your partner ($0–$10).
  • Whatever you send gets tripled before your partner receives it.
  • Your partner then decides how much to send back to you.
  • After seeing what your partner returned, rate the fairness on a 1–5 scale.
  • There are no right or wrong answers — be yourself!
Note: This game measures emotional and social traits — generosity, trust, and fairness sensitivity. There is no single "correct" way to play.
