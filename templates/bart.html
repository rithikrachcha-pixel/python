📋 How It Works

  • You have 20 balloons to inflate.
  • Each pump earns you $0.05 and grows the balloon.
  • Click "Collect & Next" at any time to bank your earnings and move on.
  • Each pump has a random chance of popping — the more pumps, the higher the risk.
  • If the balloon pops, you lose all money from that round.
  • Try to maximise your total earnings across all 20 balloons.
💡 Tip: This game measures risk tolerance — neither extremely cautious nor extremely reckless scores best. Find your balance.
