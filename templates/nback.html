📋 How It Works

  • Letters appear one at a time on the screen.
  • Press Space (or the button) when the current letter matches the one N steps back.
  • At 1-Back: match the immediately previous letter.
  • At 2-Back: match the letter 2 positions earlier. And so on.
  • Each level has 30 trials. Score ≥75% to advance to N+1-Back.
💡 Tip: Keep the sequence in your head as a running list — mentally say each letter aloud and drop the oldest one each step.
