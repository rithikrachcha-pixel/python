Tips for Assessment Games

🎯

Be Authentic

Pymetrics games measure natural traits — trying to "game" them often backfires.

⏱️

Practice Speed

SHL tests are timed. Practice until answering quickly feels natural.

🧘

Stay Calm

Anxiety hurts reaction time and working memory. Take a breath first.

🔁

Repeat Often

Cognitive assessments improve with practice. Repeat each game over several days.

Pymetrics — Risk Tolerance & Decision Making

🎈 20 balloons
💰 $0.05 per pump
⏱️ No time limit

📋 How It Works

  • You have 20 balloons to inflate.
  • Each pump earns $0.05 and grows the balloon.
  • Click "Collect & Next" to bank earnings and move on.
  • Each pump has a random chance of popping — more pumps = higher risk.

💡 Tip: Neither extremely cautious nor reckless scores best. Find your balance.

McKinsey & Company — Systems Thinking Assessment

🌿 Ecosystem building
⏱️ 25 min
🧠 Systems thinking

  • You must build a stable ecosystem by placing species (plants, herbivores, carnivores) into an environment.

Watson Glaser Critical Thinking

Clifford Chance, Allen & Overy, KPMG, EY — Law & Consulting

📋 20 questions
⏱️ 20 min
🧩 5 question types

📋 Five Question Types

  • Inference — Is this conclusion True, Probably True, Insufficient Data, Probably False, or False?
  • Assumptions — Is this assumption made in the statement? (Yes / No)
  • Deductions — Does this conclusion follow logically? (Follows / Does Not Follow)
  • Interpretation — Does this follow beyond reasonable doubt? (Follows / Does Not Follow)
  • Evaluation — Is this argument Strong & Relevant, or Weak/Irrelevant?
💡 Base answers only on what is stated — not common knowledge. "Probably True" ≠ "True".
